1. 	fry minced meat till no longer pink
2. 	add garlic 
3. 	add fish sauce, sugar, Sambal Oelek, soy sauce, Sriracha sauce
    	fry till meat nicely browned
4. 	add carrots, fry for about 5 min
5. 	add red peppers, Jalapeno pepper, tomato sauce, 
    	cook another 5 min  
6.		add Bok Choy, cook until soft

7. 	in a separate pan, fry eggs in butter 

8. 	serve over Jasmin rice, decorate egg with Sriracha sauce
