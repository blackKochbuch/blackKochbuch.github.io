1. Preheat oven to 350
2. Fry bacon in heavy cast iron casserole
3. When crispy brown put on paper towel to get rid of fat
4. Pour off most fat

5. In rest of fat brown beef 
6. Add:
		Garlic
		Thyme
		Onion
		Carrots
		Tomato paste

7. Sprinkle over with flour
8. Slowly add the beef broth and wine, stirring well
9. Add:
		 bay leaves
		sugar
10. season with salt and pepper

11. Bake in oven for 2 hours,
12. towards end add in mushrooms
