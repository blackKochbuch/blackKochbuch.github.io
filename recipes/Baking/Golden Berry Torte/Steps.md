Boil golden berries with sugar for 1 min



Whip butter till creamy, slowly add sugar and vanilla sugar, keep beating, add egg yolks, one at a time and then slowly add flour and milk (dough will be very sticky)

Whip egg whites with 2TBs of warm water and a pinch of salt, slowly adding sugar, toward end add 1/2 tsp baking powder. Beat until stiffish.

Grease 2 springform pans very well
Divide dough and baiser in 2 portions and pour dough into pan top with baiser, sprinkle with almonds
Bake 35 - 40 min at 375 until golden brown, baiser stiff



Take sugar water from golden berries and dissolve vanilla pudding in it, add the berries
Fold in the whipped cream.

When cake cooled, spoon on top of 1 cake base and cover with other
