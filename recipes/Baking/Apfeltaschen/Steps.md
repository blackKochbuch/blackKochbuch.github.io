1. On a baking tray add a sheet of baking paper.
2. Spoon or drizzle 6 tsp of honey on the baking paper, these need to be very far apart.
3. Sprinkle cinnamon on top and add 4–5 slices of apple on top of each bit of honey. Add another teaspoon of honey.
4. Add the puff pastry rectangle piece on top and press down the edges with a fork, to seal each rectangle.
5. Brush the top of the puff pastry with egg yolk.
6. Bake for 12 mins at 180°C.
