Ingredients (makes 5 pizzas):

STARTER
2 tbsp (28.5g) starter
6 tbsp (85g) water
¾ cup + 2 tbsp (113g) unbleached bread flour

DOUGH
All sourdough starter (226g)
1.5 cups (340g) water, lukewarm
4 cups (510g) unbleached bread flour
2 tsp (14g) salt
1 tsp (3g) instant yeast
2 tbsp (28.5g) sugar OR 1.5 tbsp honey
±2 tbsp olive oil
