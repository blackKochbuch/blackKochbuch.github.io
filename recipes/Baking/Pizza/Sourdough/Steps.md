Instructions:
PREPARE STARTER
1.	Combine all ingredients and stir
2.	Let ferment for 6-8 hours at room temperature
3.	Use immediately or up to 4 days later if refrigerated
PREPARE DOUGH
4.	Combine starter (cut into ~12 pieces) and lukewarm water with hands
5.	Add flour, salt, yeast, sugar, olive oil
6.	Mix at low speed x 1 minute
7.	Let dough rest for 5 minutes
8.	Mix at low-medium with dough hook x 3 minutes (until smooth and supple)
9.	Spread olive oil on work surface and stretch/ fold dough
a.	Reach under front end of the dough, stretch it out, then fold it back on top of the dough
b.	Repeat with back end and both sides
c.	Flip the dough over and tuck into a ball
10.	Divide into 5 equal pieces
11.	Form each piece into a ball and place into a freezer ball (sprayed with oil) or onto a pan and refrigerate overnight or for up to 4 days
BAKE
12.	Remove from fridge 90 minutes before baking
a.	Stretch and round each piece into a ball, loosely cover with plastic wrap, and let rest at room temperature
13.	Preheat oven to maximum temperature
14.	When ready to assemble/ bake shape the dough into disks
a.	If the dough starts to resist and shrink back set it on your floured work surface to rest for 1-2 minutes
b.	Should be about 10-12 inches in diameter, thicker at edges than in the centre
15.	Top the pizza as desired
16.	Bake on floured baking stone (heated) or pan covered with parchment paper
17.	Bake for 4 minutes, then rotate the pizza
18.	Bake until edges puffed up and golden brown to slightly charred (approximately 5-7 minutes in total)
