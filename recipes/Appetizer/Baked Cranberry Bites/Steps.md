Preheat oven to 375
Spray mini muffin tin with non stick cooking spray
Unroll one sheet of puff pastry and cut the sheet into 16 squares (4X 4 rows)
Then place squares in prepared tin
Cut brie into cubes and place one cube in the centre of each muffin tin
Spoon a small amount of cranberry sauce over the brie (careful not to overfill)

Bake 15-18 minutes

Garnish and serve
