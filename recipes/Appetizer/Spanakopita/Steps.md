Fry cut onions in butter
Season with pepper, nutmeg
Blanch spinach 
Squeeze out water
Add to onion mix
Add feta cheeses, soy sauce, eggs, dill

Roll out puff pastry 
Butter casserole pan (20 X 30 cm)

Fill with spinach mix
Cover with puff pastry
Sprinkle with sesame seeds

Cut before baking at 400 degree for 40 min
