Preheat oven to 350

Bring a big pot pf water to boil
Add 3 TBS Salt 
Add 3 cups of Basmati Rice

Boil for 5 Minutes

Peal and cut potatoes
Spread 2 Tbs Olive Oil in baking dish
Mix in salt, pepper, coriander/ cumin

Spread potatoes slices on it, rub in spiced oil

Mix a bit of rice with yoghurt
Spread over potatoes
Follow with beans ( softened, might have to be boiled for 5 minutes)
Sprinkle with dill

Mix rest of rice with garlic

Spread rice
Beans
Dill

In alternating layers

Top with rice

Pour 4 TBS of melted butter over 
Sprinkle with saffron water

Cover with aluminum foil

Bake for an hour or Moore until steam builds up
It should look golden

Let rest for some minutes
