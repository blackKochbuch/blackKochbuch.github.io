1. 		Preheat oven to 375
2. 		Pat duck breast dry
3. 		Salt duck breast, lots of salt on skin, less on meat side
4. 		Pan fry duck breast, skin side down on medium heat, 
			it should sizzle but not spit wildly, drain extra fat,
			 until golden brown,  about 15 min
5. 		Turn over, so that skin is up
6. 		Transfer into oven, about 15 min
7. 		To cross skin, fry again on stovetop, skin down for another 10 min

8.			Gravy:
	⁃	Drain fat off pan.
	⁃	In the the drippings of pan boil:
						red wine, 
						pepper, 
						thyme, 
						whipping cream
	⁃	stir, loosening burnt in yummy duck stuff, 
	⁃	add cornstarch dissolved in water
	⁃	add chicken stock if it needs more salt


9. 		Serve with red cabbage, cranberry sauce, apple sauce, 
			potatoes (mashed, sweet, yams, whatever), chestnuts
