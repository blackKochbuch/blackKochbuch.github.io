
Preheat oven to 425
Oil baking sheet and salt and pepper
Put potato half face down on sheet

Bake for 35 minutes, turn around and continue baking till soft

Gently press down on each with a fork to mash up  a bit
Then top with crumble

For the Crumble:
Chop all ingredients (including arugula)  and mix



For the Dressing:
Whisk all ingredients together and sprinkle over potato and crumble
