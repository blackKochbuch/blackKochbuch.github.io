Wash fennel, cut off the sliver of dried end and the fronds
Quater, put in a TBS of water with salt, mix in pepper, olive oil

Oil pan, arrange in single layer, pour over liquid, roast with aluminum cover for 30 min at 450, hen pout off liquid, brown for 15 min on each side


Fry bacon till crisp,

Boil linguine

Fry minced meat, seasoned with soya sauce, samba olek

Crisp nuts, mix with fennel fronds, cut small, parsley, cranberries

Mix all
