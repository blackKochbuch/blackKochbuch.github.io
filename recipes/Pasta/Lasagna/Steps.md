1. Make Spaghetti sauce
		see Spaghetti recipe

2. Make Bechamel sauce:
	1. 	Melt butter in a small pot
	2. 	Have milk and wire whisk ready
	3. 	Add flour. The butter will absorb it right away
	4. 	Add  a little milk and stir
	5. 	when no longer lumpy, add more milk, and stir, stir, stir
	6. 	Continue until all milk gone and sauce thick

3. Preheat oven to 350

4. Assemble lasagna in an oven proof 9X13 casserole dish:
	1. 	a little tomato sauce
	2. 	One layer of noodles
	3. 	Tomato sauce
	4. 	Bechamel sauce
	5. 	repeat 2 -4, until all gone
	6. 	Top with lots of cheese
	7. 	Cover with aluminum foil
	8. 	Bake for 40 min
	9. 	Remove foil
	10. 	Bake for another 15 min, until cheese melted and golden brown
