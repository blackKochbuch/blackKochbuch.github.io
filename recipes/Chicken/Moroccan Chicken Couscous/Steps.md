1.  Preheat oven to 375 F degrees
2.  Combine all the Moroccan spice blend ingredients 
3.  Rub the chicken breasts with at least 4 Tbs of 
      the Moroccan spice blend

4.  In oven safe pot heat olive oil over medium heat
5.  Sear  chicken until browned on both sides
6.  Remove chicken

7.  To drippings add:
		onions, garlic
8.  Fry until softened

9.  Add
		carrots
 		peppers
		fire roasted tomatoes 
		chicken broth
		couscous
10. Stir well
11. Add kale 
12. Bring to a boil
13. Lay chicken on top
14. Cover the pot with a lid and place in oven

15. Cook for 30 min
16. Remove lid 

17. Cook for at least another 30 min
