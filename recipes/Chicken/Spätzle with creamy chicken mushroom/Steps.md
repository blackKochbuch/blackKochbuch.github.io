1. Fry the chicken in non stick pan (no oil needed)
2. When no longer pink, add Sambal Oelek, soy sauce and onions 
    (garlic if you don’t use Boursin cheese)
3. When soy sauce absorbed and onions soft, add the Boursin cheese 
4. Melt the cheese, mix it with meat 
5. Add mushrooms and pepper
6. add white wine, chicken broth, whipping cream and flour/water mix
7. Bring to a short boil, so the sauce thickens

Serve with Spätzle, cheese, and something green
