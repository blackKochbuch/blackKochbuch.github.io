1. preheat oven to 375 degrees
2. arrange fish filets in oven proof casserole dish.
3. pour over oyster sauce and rub in on all sides
4. bake about 20 min
5. make sauce:
			1. heat olive oil in pan
			2. add garlic
			3. melt peanut butter
			4. add:
	⁃	peppers, cut in pieces
	⁃	ginger
	⁃	oyster sauce
	⁃	brown sugar
	⁃	rice vinegar
	⁃	basil seasoning paste
	⁃	water
			5. when peppers almost done, add tomatoes
6. pour sauce over fish
7. bake till fish is white and flakey
8. decorate with fresh basil
9. serve with (coconut) rice
