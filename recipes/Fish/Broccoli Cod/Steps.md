1. preheated oven to 375
2. arrange cod in oven proof casserole dish
3. rub all sides of fish with oyster sauce
4. wash and cut broccoli in bite sized pieces 
5. distribute over fish
6. crisscross with
 	 	sriracha
	 	maple syrup
	 	balsamic vinegar reduction
7. sprinkle with pepper
8. put in preheated oven for 40 min 
	or until fish flakey and broccoli crisp
