Preheat oven to 475
If salmon filets have skin, try and peel off by lifting edge and pulling off, if difficult, put filets skin side up on a rack and poor boiling water over the skin. Make sure boiling water drains away and does not cook fish, then pull off

Fry onion and garlic in butter until translucent
Add spinach and fry till wilted
Add cream cheese and melt in
Season with salt, pepper, dill
Add bread crumbs

Roll out puff pastry and cut so that each filet can be wrapped
Season Salmon on both sides with salt and pepper and put on puff pastry square
Spoon on spinach mix and wrap up
Turn upside down and put on backing sheet
Make egg wash, mix egg white and yolk
paint fish with eggs wash 
Cut squares into puff pastry 
Paint again
Put in oven 475 for 20 min
