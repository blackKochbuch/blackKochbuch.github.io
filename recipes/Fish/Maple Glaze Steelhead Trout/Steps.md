
1. Whisk together in a pot:

1/4 cup butter, melted
4 garlic cloves
2 TBS brown sugar
2 TBS maple syrup
2 TBS mustard
2 TBS soys sauce
2 TBS rice vinegar

2. Bring to boil
3. Pour over fish in alu - tent
4. Preheat oven to 400, let fish marinate till preheated
5. Bake for  10 minutes for every 1” of thickness + additional 10 minutes, 
so 20 minutes for 1”, 30 minutes for 2” and so on
