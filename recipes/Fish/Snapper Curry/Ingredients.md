INGREDIENTS

  
for the curry sauce
	•	3 stalks lemongrass sliced
	•	1 medium red onion peeled and sliced
	•	1 tablespoon chopped ginger
	•	1 tablespoon chopped garlic
	•	2 red chilli chopped
	•	1 teaspoon coriander 
	•	3 medium tomatoes, chopped
	•	1 teaspoon turmeric powder
	•	½ teaspoon curry powder
	•	1 later of coconut milk
	•	3 tablespoons tamarind chutney
	•	juice of ½ lime
	•	salt to taste

for the fish
	•	600 grams red snapper fillet skin on, portioned into 8 small fillets
	•	1 tablespoon olive oil
	•	sea salt and pepper to taste

for the garnish
	•	65 grams snow peas cleaned and sliced
	•	50 grams sugar snaps cleaned and blanched
	•	1 red chilli de-seeded and finely sliced
	•	1 baby onion peeled and finely sliced
	•	1 bunch of Tarragon leaves, chopped
	•	1 bunch of dill weed, chopped
	•	1 bunch of cilantro, chopped
	•	1/4 fennel chopped
	•	sea salt to taste
