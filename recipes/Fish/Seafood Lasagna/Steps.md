Melt 1/2 cup butter in medium size pot
Cook garlic and onion in butter for a minute
Add capers, chicken broth powder, tarragon, lemon pepper, black pepper
Then add flour and slowly while constantly stirring add milk and wine
Boil for 1 minute
Turn down heat and add mozzarella
Stir until all melted and thick

Assemble lasagna:

Cheese sauce
Noodles
Cottage cheese
Fish mix

Noodels
Cheese sauce
Cottage cheese
Fish mix

And so on ending with a bit of cheese sauce on noodles

Sprinkle with parmesan cheese

Bake uncovered at 350 for 40 minutes, then let rest for 15 minutes
