Brownies:
- 2 eggs
- 42g cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp (3g) salt
- 1/2 tsp (3g) baking powder
- 6g vanilla
- 113g melted butter
- 200g sugar
- 90g flour
- 4g espresso powder
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Ganache:
- 85g 85% dark chocolate (or more/less dark)
- 2g instant coffee powder in 45g hot water
- 60g whipping cream

Note, the photo includes a tiramisu on top. It's probably better without that and you can enjoy a tiramisu on its own another time.
