Brownies:
- Mix together the eggs, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, coffee powder, and vanilla
- In a separate bowl cream together the sugar and butter
- Microwave gradually (15s at a time) until the sugar dissolves and it becomes shiny and no longer granular
- Combine the egg and sugar mixtures well
- Add flour and mix gently - do not overmix
- Add chocolate chips and fold in
- Pour into 9x9 pan and bake at 350F for 26-28min

Coffee Chocolate Ganache:
- Chop up the chocolate pretty finely
- Mix the chocolate, coffee powder, and hot water. It should all melt together
- Add in the cream and mix well until nice and smooth

Once the brownies are mostly cool, pour the ganache on top.
