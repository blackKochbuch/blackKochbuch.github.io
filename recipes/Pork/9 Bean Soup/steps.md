Rinse beans 
In a large pot soak beans in lots of water over night, at least 12 hours
Bring beans to a rapid boil for 5 minutes
Skim off scum at top
Discard water


Saute onion, garlic and cumin for about 5 minutes, then add salt, pepper, thyme and remove from heat

Fry sausages 

Add to slow cooker with tomatoes, chilies, beans, potato and stock, meat

On high heat 2 hours
Then low heat another 2 hours or till beans soft

Serve topped with cream and cilantro
