Season meat with soya sauce, sambar oelec and sriracha, maple syrup, fish sauce, sesame oil, vinegar

Let sit for a few hours

Heat 2 tablespoon oil in a large non-stick pan over medium heat. Once hot add sliced okra and a little salt. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until the slime disappears and okra starts to crisp. Remove cooked okra and set aside.

Fry meat in a separate pan

Fry onion, garlic, ginger, add spices, add meat and cherry tomatoes. Let cook till cherry tomatoes fall apart. At the very end add okra.
