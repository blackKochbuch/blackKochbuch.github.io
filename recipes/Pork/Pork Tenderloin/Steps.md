Preheat oven to 350
heat oil in pan and fry tenderloin till browned on all sides
Add soya sauce and brown a little longer, so that meat has absorbed soya sauce
Transfer in casserole, make a cut lengthwise (depth about 1/2 of whole meat depth)   and fill/ cover with tomato paste
season with pepper 
Pour whipping cream over the meat
Sprinkle cheese on top
Roast in oven
